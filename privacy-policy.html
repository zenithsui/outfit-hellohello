Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use our online store.

This website is operated by Amit, doing business as 26Football.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data transparently and securely.

02

What Information We Collect

2.1 — Information you provide to us

We may collect and process the following personal information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Shipping and billing address
  • Payment information
  • Order details and purchase history
  • Any communications you direct to us regarding your gear or jersey orders

2.2 — Information collected automatically

When you access our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Device type and browser information
  • Approximate location data
  • Pages visited and browsing behavior
  • Cookies and tracking data

2.3 — Information from third parties

We may receive information from:

  • Payment processors (e.g., payment confirmations)
  • Shipping partners (delivery tracking)
  • E-commerce and hosting applications (e.g., Shopify, WordPress, or similar platforms)
03

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data to:

  • Process and fulfill your order
  • Deliver products (including jerseys and apparel) and manage shipping
  • Provide customer support
  • Communicate with you about your order
  • Process payments and prevent fraud
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data to third parties, nor share the profiles with others except as detailed under Section 5 below.

04

Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable (for UK and EU users), we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract (to fulfill your order)
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Legitimate interests (running, managing, and securing our e-commerce business)
  • Consent (where mandated, for example, in relation to cookies)
05

Sharing Your Information

We share your personal data only when necessary and in connection with operating our store, including with:

  • E-commerce and website platform providers
  • Payment processors and facilitators
  • Shipping and logistics partners
  • IT and service providers supporting our store
  • Legal or regulatory authorities where required

All third parties are required to handle your data securely and in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

06

International Data Transfers

As we operate on the internet, your personal data may be transferred and processed outside your country of residence. Where required, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to maintain the security of your data.

07

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill your order
  • Meet legal, tax, or accounting requirements
  • Resolve disputes or enforce agreements
08

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • The right to access your personal data
  • The right to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to restrict or object to processing
  • The right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at amit@26football.com.
